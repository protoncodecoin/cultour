Privacy Policy

CulTour-Gh values your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect
  • Name, email address, phone number (when you contact us or book a tour)
  • Device and browser information
  • Website usage data (via cookies or analytics)
How We Use Your Information
  • To respond to inquiries or bookings
  • To improve our services and website performance
  • To send updates or newsletters (if you opt in)
Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners who help operate the website or process bookings.

Cookies

We use cookies to analyze website traffic and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to request, correct, or delete your personal information. For privacy questions, contact us at cultourgh@gmail.com.

Cookie Policy

CulTour-Gh uses cookies to improve your experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential: Needed for site functionality
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. via Google Analytics)
Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features may not function correctly without cookies.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

At CulTour-Gh, we aim to provide memorable and reliable experiences. Please read our cancellation policy below:

Cancellations
  • More than 7 days before the tour: Full refund
  • Within 3–6 days: 50% refund
  • Less than 48 hours: No refund
No-Shows

If you don’t show up at the scheduled time without notice, no refund will be issued.

Tour Changes

We reserve the right to modify or cancel a tour due to weather or safety concerns. In such cases, you will be offered a reschedule or full refund.

Contact for Cancellations

Email us at cultourgh@gmail.com with your booking reference.

Responsible Tourism Policy

At CulTour-Gh, we believe tourism should benefit both travelers and local communities. We are committed to:

  • Supporting local businesses, artisans, and guides
  • Promoting cultural respect and preservation
  • Minimizing environmental impact through sustainable practices
  • Educating visitors about local customs and traditions

By joining our tours, you agree to respect local cultures, people, and the environment. Let’s travel responsibly together.

Accessibility Statement

CulTour-Gh is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We are working to improve our site's compatibility with screen readers and other assistive technologies.

If you encounter an accessibility issue, please contact us at cultourgh@gmail.com and we will do our best to help.
